About

Aya Aly is a leading sustainable finance and climate specialist in Egypt, currently serving as Manager of the Sustainable Development Department at The Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA). With a Master of Public Policy from The American University in Cairo and specialized training from Institut Barcelona d'Estudis Internacionals and Frankfurt School of Finance & Management, she brings international expertise to Egypt's financial sector transformation.

Her research on corporate green bond market development and extensive work on ESG integration have positioned her as a thought leader in sustainable finance across the Middle East and North Africa region. Aya has represented Egypt in international forums, contributed to regulatory frameworks for sustainable finance, and actively works to bridge the gap between climate policy and financial market development.
